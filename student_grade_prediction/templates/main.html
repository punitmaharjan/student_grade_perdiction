Our Mission

Our mission is to help students, parents, and educators make informed decisions about academic performance by predicting student grades based on various factors. “Student Grade Prediction” is a web-based application designed with the intent of helping educators to predict the student’s final grade based on range of factors like study hours, previous grades, etc. and identifying risks related to students to provide them with necessary support to improve their grades.

How It Works

Using linear regression algorithm and statistical analysis, our application analyzes student data like previous grades (G1 and G2), mother's education, and number of failures to predict the final grade of a student. By providing accurate predictions, we aim to assist in identifying areas of improvement and supporting academic success.
